About me:

I am "retired" optometrist and Contact Lens Specialist. In my previous work I was very detailed, emphatetic, understanding and tried to fit best contact lenses to my patients. After a while I saw that this kind of work is not form me - very monotonous, stressful and the most important - I wasn't feeling it anymore. I was trying to find something challenging, creative and useful. Discovering Front-End Development was a blessing! For now I'm on the beggining of my adventure with programming, but I know that I would love to create esthetic and intuitive Web Sites.
